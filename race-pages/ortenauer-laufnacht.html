Willkommen beim Running Team Ortenau! Let's talk about the 10. Ortenauer Laufnacht, where the Kreiswaldlaufmeisterschaften took place in Rheinbischofsheim on March 10, 2024. This event saw the participation of Björn Treyer, Recep Arslan, and the entire Utz family, including Verena, Marco, and their children. This competitive race featured 10 km, 5 km, and 1 km distances, all on the road. As race director, I am pleased to report that the race had a strong turnout and enthusiastic participation from runners of all levels. The beautiful course in Rheinbischofsheim provided the perfect backdrop for a memorable race experience. We look forward to continuing to host events that bring together the running community and offer a variety of race distances to suit every athlete's skill level and goals. We are proud of the dedication and sportsmanship displayed by all participants, and we hope to see you at our future events to keep pushing boundaries and achieving new personal bests.
