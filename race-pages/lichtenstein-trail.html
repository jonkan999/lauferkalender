Liechtenstein Trail: Crossing the whole country and connecting all of Liechtenstein’s municipalities. Celebrating the country's 300th birthday, this race offers runners the chance to take on a 75km hiking route that showcases the nation's stunning landscapes and rich heritage. As the world's sixth-smallest nation, Liechtenstein may not see high tourist numbers, but it is a destination perfect for adventurous souls. Located in the heart of the Central Alps, the country boasts some of the most beautiful mountain views in Europe. This race offers a unique opportunity for runners to explore Liechtenstein in a way that most tourists never get to. With a variety of race categories and distances, including an 11km road race, participants can select the perfect challenge to suit their abilities and enjoy the breathtaking scenery along the way. Don't miss this chance to experience the magic of Liechtenstein on foot.
