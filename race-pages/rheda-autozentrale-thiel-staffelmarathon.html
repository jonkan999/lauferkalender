Ladies and gentlemen, welcome to the annual Münster Marathon! The race will take place in our beautiful city, offering participants the opportunity to run through the streets and experience the lively atmosphere. The marathon is the main event, with runners taking on the 26.2-mile challenge, while there are also opportunities for half marathon and 10k races. This road race is suitable for all levels of runners, whether you're a seasoned marathoner or a first-time participant. The route will showcase some of Münster's most iconic landmarks, providing a scenic backdrop for the race. Spectators can line the streets to cheer on the runners, creating an electric atmosphere throughout the city. It's a celebration of fitness, community, and achievement, as participants push themselves to reach their personal goals. Join us for an unforgettable day of running, camaraderie, and triumph as we come together for the Münster Marathon!
